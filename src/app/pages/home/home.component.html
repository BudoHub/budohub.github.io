BudoHub Martial Arts Management

About Us

An integrated payment, membership, and scheduling system for martial arts schools.

Written by developers that share a passion for technology and the martial arts. We see a need for a system that can enable martial artists from around the globe to connect and share their knowledge and experiences. We believe that the best way to do this is to provide a platform that is easy to use, affordable, secure and most importanly respects the members privacy above all else.

To that end we are actively developing this platform and are rapidly approaching an inital alpha release.
